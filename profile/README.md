# Vector Works Architect – Professional BIM & CAD for Architecture

A powerful, open‑source **Vector Works Architect** solution designed to modernize architectural design and building information modeling. This **Vector Works Architect** software provides a comprehensive environment for creating, managing, and documenting complex architectural projects, allowing professionals to focus on creativity rather than repetitive drafting tasks.

## About Vector Works Architect
**Vector Works Architect** is a professional‑grade BIM and CAD platform built specifically for architects, landscape designers, and interior planners.  
It provides access to hybrid design workflows (2D/3D), parametric objects, intelligent building components, and automated documentation.  
**Vector Works Architect** offers seamless integration with IFC, DWG, DXF, and PDF formats, enabling deep collaboration, real‑time model analysis, and construction‑ready output.  
**Vector Works Architect** remains flexible and powerful, making it an essential tool for both small studios and large AEC firms.

## Key Features of Vector Works Architect
- **Hybrid BIM Workflow** – Combine 2D drafting and 3D modeling within **Vector Works Architect** without switching tools.  
- **Parametric Building Components** – Use smart walls, slabs, roofs, and columns in **Vector Works Architect** to automate changes.  
- **Landscape & Site Design** – **Vector Works Architect** includes hardscape, planting, irrigation, and terrain modeling tools.  
- **Construction Documentation** – Generate sections, elevations, schedules, and details directly from **Vector Works Architect** models.  
- **Data Visualization** – Apply styles, textures, and lighting using **Vector Works Architect** render engines (Redshift, Realistic, Artistic).  
- **IFC & Industry Collaboration** – **Vector Works Architect** imports/exports IFC4, DWG, DXF, Revit, and SketchUp files.  
- **Worksheet & Reporting** – Create material takeoffs, door/window schedules, and area calculations inside **Vector Works Architect**.  
- **Energy & Performance Analysis** – **Vector Works Architect** integrates solar exposure, heating/cooling load, and carbon footprint tools.  
- **Scripting & Automation** – Extend **Vector Works Architect** with VectorScript, Python, or Marionette visual scripting.  
- **Multi‑User Collaboration** – **Vector Works Architect** supports referenced files, project sharing, and cloud workspaces.

## Why Choose Vector Works Architect

**Vector Works Architect** eliminates the traditional barrier between 2D drafting and 3D modeling. Unlike rigid BIM tools, **Vector Works Architect** allows you to start in 2D and later extrude to 3D, or begin in 3D and auto‑generate 2D documents. This hybrid approach makes **Vector Works Architect** ideal for renovations, complex geometries, and fast iterative design.  

Professional firms rely on **Vector Works Architect** for stadiums, museums, residential complexes, and urban planning because of its unmatched flexibility. Every command inside **Vector Works Architect** can be customized, scripted, or automated.  

From schematic design to construction administration, **Vector Works Architect** delivers consistent, data‑rich, and visually stunning results. The learning curve is smooth, yet **Vector Works Architect** scales to handle multi‑story buildings with thousands of objects.  

Join thousands of architects who have switched to **Vector Works Architect** for its speed, openness, and lack of subscription lock‑in. **Vector Works Architect** respects your data, your workflow, and your creative freedom.
